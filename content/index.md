Hi, My name is Boluwatife Olabiran. I am a PhD student at the Florida State University with the Florida A&M University-Florida State University College of Engineering. I am currently working on the following:
* A unimodal Transformer based end-to-end autonomous driver
* Autonomous racing against dynamic obstacles using Model Predictive Control
* Visual Servoing for autonomous vehicles in the presence of obstacles.
