**This section can be done either before or after the [[Hardware Setup]]. However, it is recommended to complete the [[Hardware Setup]] before proceeding for immediate testing.**
By the end of this section, you should have a Jetson Orin Nano (and optionally a remote x86_64 computer) ready for full autonomy, i.e teleoperation, sensing, mapping, localization, perception, planning, [[Real-Time Trajectory Tracking]] and low-level motion control.
## Outline
1. Complete the steps detailed in the [[Software Setup]]
2. Setup sensors by following the instructions detailed in the [[ii. Sensors]] section.
3. (optional) Setup communication with a remote PC as described in [[iii. Remote PC Communication]]
